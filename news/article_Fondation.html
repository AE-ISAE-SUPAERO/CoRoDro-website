Thank to Fondation Supaero

Thanks
February 22, 2021

Mathéo Fouchet

We would like to thank the Fondation ISAE – SUPAERO for their bountiful financial help, otherwise this project wouldn’t have been possible. The Fondation ISAE – SUPAERO is an association based on the donation mainly given by former student of the institute

Thanks to grant campaign the Fondation organizes every year, most of the student projects developed within ISAE - SUPAERO are now possible such as IGLUNA and our CoRoDro team. This is why we are really grateful to those who trusted our CoRoDro project. To all the members of the Fondation : THANK YOU.
